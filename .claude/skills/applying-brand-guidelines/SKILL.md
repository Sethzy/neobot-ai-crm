# Sunder Brand Guidelines Skill

This skill ensures all generated documents adhere to Sunder brand standards for consistent, professional communication.

## Brand Identity

### Company: Sunder Inc.
**Tagline**: "Document chaos, intelligently solved."
**Industry**: AI Document Processing

## Visual Standards

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors**:
- **NeoBot Green**: #2D6A4F (RGB: 45, 106, 79) - Headers, primary buttons, CTAs
- **NeoBot Green Light**: #40916C (RGB: 64, 145, 108) - Accent, hover states
- **NeoBot Green Dark**: #1B4332 (RGB: 27, 67, 50) - Dark variants, body text on light
- **White**: #FFFFFF - Backgrounds, reverse text

**Secondary Colors**:
- **Success Green**: #19A249 (RGB: 25, 162, 73) - Positive metrics
- **Warning Amber**: #D87708 (RGB: 216, 119, 8) - Cautions
- **Info Blue**: #2762EA (RGB: 39, 98, 234) - Informational
- **Content Gray**: #1F2937 (RGB: 31, 41, 55) - Body text
- **Border Gray**: #E4E4E7 (RGB: 228, 228, 231) - Borders
- **Light Gray**: #F8F9FA (RGB: 248, 249, 250) - Alternating rows

### Typography

**Font Families**:
- **Primary Sans**: Figtree - UI, body text, data
- **Display Serif**: Playfair Display - Headlines, feature titles
- **Logo Wordmark**: DM Sans Bold

**Font Hierarchy**:
- **H1**: 32pt, Playfair Display Medium, NeoBot Green
- **H2**: 24pt, Figtree Semibold, Content Gray
- **H3**: 18pt, Figtree Semibold, Content Gray
- **Body**: 11pt, Figtree Regular, Content Gray
- **Caption**: 9pt, Figtree Regular, Border Gray

### Logo Usage

- Position: Top-left corner on first page/slide
- Size: 120px width (maintain aspect ratio)
- Clear space: Minimum 20px padding on all sides
- Color: NeoBot Green (#2D6A4F) on light backgrounds
- Never distort, rotate, or apply effects

## Document Standards

### PowerPoint Presentations

**Slide Templates**:
1. **Title Slide**: Company logo, presentation title, date, presenter
2. **Section Divider**: Section title with NeoBot Green background
3. **Content Slide**: Title bar with NeoBot Green background, white content area
4. **Data Slide**: For charts/graphs, maintain color palette

**Layout Rules**:
- Margins: 0.5 inches all sides
- Title position: Top 15% of slide
- Bullet indentation: 0.25 inches per level
- Maximum 6 bullet points per slide
- Charts use brand colors exclusively

### Excel Spreadsheets

**Formatting Standards**:
- **Headers**: Row 1, Bold, White text on NeoBot Green (#2D6A4F) background
- **Subheaders**: Bold, Content Gray text
- **Data cells**: Regular, Content Gray text
- **Borders**: Thin, Border Gray (#E4E4E7)
- **Alternating rows**: Light Gray (#F8F9FA) for readability

**Chart Defaults**:
- Primary series: NeoBot Green
- Secondary series: Success Green
- Gridlines: Border Gray, 0.5pt
- No 3D effects or gradients

### PDF Documents

**Page Layout**:
- **Header**: Company logo left, document title center, page number right
- **Footer**: Copyright notice left, date center, classification right
- **Margins**: 1 inch all sides
- **Line spacing**: 1.15
- **Paragraph spacing**: 12pt after

**Section Formatting**:
- Main headings: NeoBot Green, 16pt, Playfair Display bold
- Subheadings: Content Gray, 14pt, Figtree semibold
- Body text: Content Gray, 11pt, Figtree regular

## Content Guidelines

### Tone of Voice

- **Clear**: Direct, no jargon, get to the point
- **Modern**: Tech-forward, contemporary language
- **Confident**: Assertive but not arrogant
- **Helpful**: Focus on solutions and outcomes

### Standard Phrases

**Opening Statements**:
- "Sunder transforms..."
- "With Sunder, you can..."
- "Built for teams who value..."

**Closing Statements**:
- "Ready to simplify your document workflow?"
- "See how Sunder can help your team."
- "Transform your document chaos today."

### Data Presentation

**Numbers**:
- Use comma separators for thousands
- Currency: $X,XXX.XX format
- Percentages: XX.X% (one decimal)
- Dates: YYYY-MM-DD (ISO format preferred)

**Tables**:
- Headers in NeoBot Green
- Alternating row colors
- Right-align numbers
- Left-align text

## Quality Standards

### Before Finalizing

Always ensure:
1. Logo is properly placed and sized
2. All colors match brand palette exactly
3. Fonts are consistent throughout
4. No typos or grammatical errors
5. Data is accurately presented
6. Professional tone maintained

### Prohibited Elements

Never use:
- Clip art or stock photos without approval
- Comic Sans, Papyrus, or decorative fonts
- Rainbow colors or gradients
- Animations or transitions (unless specified)
- Competitor branding or references

## Application Instructions

When creating any document:
1. Start with Sunder brand colors and fonts
2. Apply appropriate template structure
3. Include logo on first page/slide
4. Use consistent formatting throughout
5. Review against brand standards
6. Ensure professional appearance

## Scripts

- `apply_brand.py`: Automatically applies brand formatting to documents
- `validate_brand.py`: Checks documents for brand compliance

## Notes

- These guidelines apply to all external communications
- Internal documents may use simplified formatting
- Special projects may have exceptions (request approval)
- Brand guidelines updated as needed - check for latest version
