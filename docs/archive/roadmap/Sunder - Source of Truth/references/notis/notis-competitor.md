# Notis — Competitor Reference

**URL**: https://notis.ai/
**Logged**: 2026-04-07
**Relevance**: Direct overlap on voice-first task automation and advisory workflows via messaging channels

## What It Is

Notis is an AI "intern" that takes action inside your existing tools (Notion, Slack, Google Workspace, etc.) via voice and text commands sent through messaging apps. No separate app — you interact through Telegram, iMessage, WhatsApp, Slack, or email.

Trusted by 17,000+ founders and operators.

## Key Features

- **Voice-first interface** — submit tasks as voice notes via Telegram, iMessage, WhatsApp, Slack, or email
- **Meeting management** — scheduling, prep, follow-ups, and call logging
- **CRM & communication** — updates Notion databases, manages email, drafts replies
- **Content generation** — blog posts, newsletters, social media, image/video generation
- **Data processing** — expense tracking, receipt OCR, PDF/Word/Excel analysis
- **Research** — web scraping and deep research
- **800+ integrations** — Notion-native, Google Workspace, custom webhooks
- **Automation workflows** — scheduled tasks, webhook triggers, integration-based automations
- **Second Brain** — optional Notion template for organizing tasks, notes, and projects

Usage is task-based (~200 / ~600 / ~1,500 tasks per month per tier).

## Positioning

> "More capable than an EA. More affordable than an intern."

Emphasizes **action over conversation** — differentiates from chatbots by actually doing work inside your tools. Notion-native positioning, strong community of founders and operators. Privacy-first (no training on user data).

## Why This Matters for Sunder

- **Same channel strategy** — Telegram + messaging-first UI is core to both; Notis also supports iMessage and WhatsApp which we don't yet
- **Similar output framing** — "we do the work, not just answer questions" mirrors Sunder's autopilot pitch
- **Different memory model** — Notis defers to Notion as the Second Brain; Sunder's memory compounds in proprietary per-client files (SOUL.md, MEMORY.md) — our moat
- **Broad vs. vertical** — Notis is horizontal (any founder/operator), Sunder is vertical (advisory sales: real estate, insurance, financial planning) — sharper ICP gives us better context engineering
- **No approval gate** — Notis doesn't surface an internal/external safety distinction; Sunder's approval gate is a trust-building differentiator
- **Pricing parity** — Pro tiers are equivalent ($20/mo). Sunder should position on depth of client memory and vertical specificity, not price
