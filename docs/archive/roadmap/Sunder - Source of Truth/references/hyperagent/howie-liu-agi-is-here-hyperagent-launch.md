## Core Thesis
Models shifted from assistants to autonomous agents. Intelligence is no longer the bottleneck — **harnessing it is**. The missing piece is the complete system: orchestration, tools, memory, skills, deployment, management.

Analogy: processors needed Apple/Microsoft to become useful. Models need the same.

## HyperAgent's Four Pillars
1. **Capable agents** — real compute environment, browser, 100s of integrations
2. **Skill learning** — agents accumulate domain expertise over time
3. **Org deployment** — one-click Slack deployment; agents act proactively
4. **Fleet management** — automated scoring, A/B testing, continuous improvement

## Key Quote
> "The orchestration, the tools, the memory, the skills, the ability to deploy and manage agents across an entire organization."
