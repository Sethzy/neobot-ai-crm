# Prio — Competitor Reference

**URL**: https://prio.sh/
**Logged**: 2026-04-09
**Relevance**: Very close overlap — AI personal agent with approval gates, email/calendar/task management, and explicit executive-assistant positioning for solo professionals

## What It Is

Prio is a cloud-based AI personal agent that manages email, calendar, tasks, and workflows through a single chat interface. Users describe what they need, and Prio drafts actions for approval before executing. Targets solo founders, agency owners, and busy professionals as an EA replacement without hiring full-time staff. EU-based (GDPR compliant, EU data residency).

## Key Features

- **Email management** — triage, categorization, response drafting
- **Calendar intelligence** — smart scheduling with focus-time protection and travel buffers
- **Task handling** — creation, tracking, auto-scheduling to calendar
- **Voice interface** — dictation with transcription and action extraction
- **Daily briefings** — morning summaries of calendar, tasks, and emails
- **Approval gates** — every action requires explicit user confirmation before execution
- **Integrations** — Gmail, Outlook, Google Calendar, Slack, Notion, LinkedIn, and more

## Positioning

> Positioned as a managed-cloud alternative to self-hosted AI assistants (e.g. OpenClaw). Emphasizes security and privacy: "EU data residency, GDPR compliant, no training on user data."

Approval-first UX is a core differentiator — every action is drafted and confirmed, not auto-executed.

## Why This Matters for Sunder

- **Same approval-gate architecture** — Prio's "draft then confirm" flow mirrors Sunder's internal/external safety model almost exactly; this validates the pattern but means we can't claim it as unique
- **Horizontal vs. vertical** — Prio targets any busy professional; Sunder is vertical (advisory sales) with deep per-client memory — our CRM awareness and SOUL.md/MEMORY.md context are things Prio cannot replicate
- **No CRM or client memory** — Prio manages the practitioner's own calendar and inbox but has no model of their clients; Sunder's compounding per-client files are the moat
- **No autopilot** — Prio requires a user prompt to act; Sunder runs autonomously on cron/triggers (pulse, meeting follow-ups, inbound triage) without the practitioner initiating anything
- **Pricing ceiling** — €79/mo Pro is roughly $85; Sunder should be comfortable pricing at or above this given vertical depth and autonomous runs
- **EU GDPR angle** — Prio explicitly markets EU residency; if Sunder targets European advisors this is a gap to address
